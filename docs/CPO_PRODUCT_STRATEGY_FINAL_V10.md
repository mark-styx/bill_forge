## Executive Summary

Bill Forge enters the AP automation market with a focused thesis: **mid-market companies (50-500 employees) are underserved**. Enterprise platforms are overbuilt and overpriced. SMB tools lack workflow sophistication. We win in the gap.

### The Bill Forge Position

**For:** AP managers and controllers at mid-market companies (50-500 employees, 300-5,000 invoices/month)

**Who:** Are frustrated with manual data entry, approval bottlenecks, and overpriced legacy tools

**We offer:** A modern, fast, modular AP platform with 90%+ OCR accuracy, email-based approvals, and usage-based pricing

**Unlike:** Legacy players (Palette, AvidXchange) that are slow and dated, or SMB tools (BILL) that can't scale

**We:** Process invoices in seconds, let approvers act from their inbox without login, and charge for what you use - not per seat

### Strategic Bets (Ranked)

| # | Bet | Hypothesis | Q1 Validation |
|---|-----|------------|---------------|
| 1 | **Speed wins** | Sub-second UI = visible differentiation | NPS cites speed in top 3 reasons |
| 2 | **Email approvals** | No-login approvals cut cycle time 50% | >50% of approvals via email |
| 3 | **Usage pricing** | No seat tax = faster adoption | >80% prospects prefer our model |
| 4 | **Local OCR option** | Privacy buyers exist (healthcare, legal) | >20% choose local-only mode |
| 5 | **Modularity** | Buy what you need, expand later | >30% expand modules in Year 1 |

Unlike legacy solutions that are slow, expensive, and require IT resources, Bill Forge offers **instant OCR, one-click approvals from email, and usage-based pricing** that scales with your business.

### Q2: OCR Accuracy Threshold for Queue Routing?

**Recommendation: Three-tier confidence routing**

| Confidence | Queue | User Experience |
|------------|-------|-----------------|
| >=85% | AP Queue (auto-route) | Green indicators, proceed to workflow |
| 70-84% | Review Queue | Yellow indicators, verify flagged fields |
| <70% | Error Queue | Red indicators, full manual entry |

**Implementation:**
- Overall confidence = weighted average of fields
- Weights: amount (30%), vendor (25%), invoice# (20%), date (15%), currency (10%)
- Display per-field confidence for targeted review
- Collect corrections as training data
- Allow tenant-configurable thresholds

**Recommendation: QuickBooks Online**

| ERP | Priority | Market Fit | API Quality | Timeline |

### Q5: Multi-Currency Handling?

**MVP Approach:**
- Capture currency from invoice as metadata
- Support: USD, EUR, GBP, CAD
- Convert for display using daily rates (Open Exchange Rates)
- Store both original and converted amounts
- Send base currency to ERP
- Flag variance >2% for review
- **Defer full multi-currency GL to Phase 3**

### Q6: Pricing Model That Resonates?

**Research Insights:**
1. Per-user pricing universally disliked
2. Volume-based = perceived value correlation
3. Predictability matters for budgeting
4. Transparency builds trust vs. "call for quote"

**Bill Forge Formula:**
- Base tier with included volume (predictability)
- Reasonable overage (flexibility, no cliff)
- Zero per-user fees (no friction for approvers)
- Published pricing (trust)
- Annual prepay discount (cash flow)
